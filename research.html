Research

  • At COSSEE, we strive to answer fundamental questions (e.g., life-history evolution, trans-generational effects) as well as applied questions (e.g., evolutionary medicine, environmental change, eco-toxicology). We tackle inter-disciplinary questions using a wide range of techniques such as behavioural observations and experiments, molecular and physiological tools, as well as developing new statistical methods. We increasingly work with computational models, Big Data, and research synthesis (collating evidence from multiple primary studies). We always strive to produce robust and transparent cutting-edge research.
