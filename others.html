Other Stuff

Other things we do that are related to Open Science.
SOCIETIES
Many members of our research groups are members of the grassroots Open Science societies:

SORTEE - Society for Open Reliable Transparent Ecology and Evolutionary biology was co-efounded by Shinichi Nakagawa, malgorzata Lagisz, Rose O'Dea and our colleagues from other research groups.  It aims to enable its members to work to improve methods and practices in ecology, evolutionary biology, and related disciplines. It organises international virtual conferences annually and other events. Members of our research group hold positions at many of the Committees in this society and co-organise events.

AIMOS - the Association for Interdisciplinary Meta-Research and Open Science) seeks to advance the interdisciplinary field of meta-research by organising international conferences and other events. Members of our research group hold at the Board of this society and co-organise events

PREPRINT SERVER
EcoEvoRxiv - a server for preprints in Ecology and Evolution (and related disciplines) was co-established by Shinichi Nakagawa, with sevferal other group members and collaborators contributing in various roles.
